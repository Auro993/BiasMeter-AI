About BiasMeter AI

BiasMeter AI is an ethical AI bias detection platform developed for the Student Global Summit. Our mission is to help identify and mitigate biases in AI models to promote fairness and equity in artificial intelligence.

How It Works

Upload a CSV file with gender and decision data. Our system analyzes selection rates across different groups and calculates bias scores to help you identify potential discrimination.
